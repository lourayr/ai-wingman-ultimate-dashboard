AI Income Workshop: The 14-Day Sequence System
Quick Orientation
The AI Income Workshop VIP Session 5 teaches a structured 14-day Instagram Story sequence for launching digital products with creators. This system matters because it transforms passive audiences into buyers through a proven psychological framework. The core system uses three phases (Warm-Up, Value Delivery, Open Cart) to guide audience members from awareness to purchase. The primary leverage point is using specialized AI tools like Ghostwriter OS to automate the entire sequence creation while maintaining the creator's voice.
Core Framework: The 14-Day Sequence
* Structure: A 14-day Instagram Story sequence designed to convert a creator's audience into buyers
* Three Phases: Warm-Up (Days 1-3), Value Delivery (Days 4-9), Open Cart (Days 10-14)
* Psychology: Builds trust first, delivers value second, opens sales third—matching how people naturally make buying decisions
* Creator Role: Appears as the face/voice while you operate behind the scenes as the Shadow Operator
* Automation: Uses specialized AI tools (Ghostwriter OS, Synthesize) to generate the entire sequence with minimal manual work
* Platform: Delivers through Instagram Stories where audiences are already actively engaged
* Revenue Split: Automated through Whop's revenue split feature (no manual invoicing)
Campaign System Definition
* Campaign Objective: Convert a creator's existing audience into digital product buyers
* Success Metric: First-time product sales (typically $3,000-$10,000+ depending on audience size)
* Primary Audience: Warm audience who already follows the creator and watches their stories regularly
* Exclusion Criteria: Cold traffic, non-followers, inactive followers who don't watch stories
* Channel Assumption: Instagram Stories as primary channel (where the audience already engages)
* Repetition Logic: Structure remains constant, content customized to creator's voice/niche
* Kill Conditions: Low story view rates, minimal engagement with early sequence posts
* Iteration Triggers: Low conversion rate despite good engagement, audience confusion in DMs
Campaign Asset Extraction
Offer Angles & Positioning
* Transformation Focus: Position as the solution to a problem the audience already knows they have
* Exclusivity: Limited-time access to the creator's system/knowledge/methodology
* Social Proof: Show others implementing the creator's advice with results
* Urgency: Time-limited enrollment, limited spots, or special bonuses for early action
Headline & Hook Frameworks
* Day 1 (Survey): "What's the biggest thing stopping you from [desired outcome]?"
* Day 4 (Opportunity): "Here's what nobody tells you about [topic]..."
* Day 10 (Launch): "Today is the day! The [product name] is now open!"
* Day 14 (Close): "Last chance! Cart closes tonight at midnight."
Audience Segmentation Logic
* Awareness Level: Segment by how aware they are of their problem
* Engagement Level: Segment by story views and DM interactions
* Action Takers: Identify those who respond to questions/polls
* Previous Buyers: Special messaging for those who've purchased before
Messaging Pillars
* Problem Amplification: Highlight pain points in Warm-Up phase
* Authority Building: Showcase creator's expertise in Value Delivery
* Social Proof: Feature success stories throughout sequence
* Objection Handling: Address common concerns pre-emptively
* Scarcity/Urgency: Create legitimate reasons to act now

* DM Scripts: For handling inquiries, objections, and closing sales
* Whop Sales Page: Product description, benefits, testimonials

Variable Map

Pitfalls to Avoid
* Skipping the Warm-Up Phase: Jumping straight to selling without building trust first
* Generic Content: Not customizing the sequence to the creator's voice and audience
* Overwhelming the Creator: Giving them too many tasks or technical responsibilities
* Ignoring Audience Feedback: Not adapting based on responses during the sequence
* Poor Timing: Launching when the audience or creator has low engagement
