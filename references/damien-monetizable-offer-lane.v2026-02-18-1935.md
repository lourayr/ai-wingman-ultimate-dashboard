## 2) Intake Form (compressed)
1. What are the top 3 operational pain points?
2. Which workflows repeat weekly and consume the most hours?
3. Which tools are currently in use (CRM, PM, communication, docs)?
4. What is the highest-priority business outcome in 90 days?
5. Any compliance/security constraints?
6. Team capacity for implementation (hours/week)?
7. What has already been attempted with AI?
8. What cannot break during implementation?

---

## 3) Discovery Script (60-minute call)

### Opening (5 min)
- Confirm outcome, scope, and success definition.

### Diagnostic (35 min)
- Current-state workflow walk-through
- Bottleneck/failure-point mapping
- Data handoff points and manual overhead
- Decision latency and approval loops

### Prioritization (15 min)
- Score opportunities by impact x effort x risk
- Select top 3 actions for sprint

### Close (5 min)
- Confirm deliverables, timing, owner, next meeting
