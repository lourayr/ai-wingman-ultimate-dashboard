**Priority Rule:** Address the highest-scoring category first. If two categories tie, prioritize the one that compounds (Time Lost and Repetitive compound fastest).

## DASHBOARD DESIGN PRINCIPLES

When designing the "Calm Operations Dashboard" for any client:

1. **Single Pane of Glass:** one view that answers "how is the business doing right now?"
2. **Three Zones:**
   - Zone A (top): Critical metrics and alerts (revenue, pipeline, blocked items)
   - Zone B (middle): Active workflows and status (projects, orders, campaigns)
   - Zone C (bottom): Health indicators (team bandwidth, upcoming deadlines, system status)
3. **No Click-Throughs for Basics:** the most-needed information visible without navigation
4. **Color Coding:** green (healthy), amber (attention needed), red (action required)
5. **Update Frequency:** real-time where possible, daily minimum, never stale
6. **Mobile-Ready:** founders check dashboards on phones; design for small screens first
